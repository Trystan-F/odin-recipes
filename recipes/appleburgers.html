Pork Apple Burgers

Description

These burgers are a delightful twist on the classic beef patty, perfect for a quick meal. Fire up the grill and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lean pork mince
  • Apple
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Egg
  • Buns

Method

  1. Mince, apple, breadcrumbs in bowl mix well
  2. Cook burgers on grill
  3. Place burgers in a bun with your choice of toppings
