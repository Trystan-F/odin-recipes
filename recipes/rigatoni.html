Sausage Rigatoni

Description

This spicy sausage pasta is a new favourite of mine, and I'm confident that it’ll become one of yours, too. On the table in 20 mins, it’s a weeknight hero.

Ingredients

  • 6 Good-Quality Sausages
  • 2 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 Shallot
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 400g Rigatoni
  • 220ml Single Cream
  • 2 Limes
  • 40g Coriander
    •

    Method

    1. Decase and fry sausages
    2. Mince shallot and garlic
    3. Butter and minced veg into the pan
    4. Add cream and juice of both limes
    5. Cook rigatoni al dente
    6. Add pasta to sausages and veg
    7. Add coriander
    8. Serve with grated lime zest
